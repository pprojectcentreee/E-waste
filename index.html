About Us

What is E-Waste?

E-waste refers to discarded electronic devices containing toxic materials that harm the environment.

🌍

Environmental Crisis

Over 50 million tonnes of e-waste generated annually. Only 20% gets properly recycled. The rest pollutes soil, water and air.

☠️

Toxic Materials

E-waste contains lead, mercury, cadmium and brominated flame retardants that cause cancer, organ damage and birth defects.

♻️

Our Solution

GreenCycle provides doorstep collection, proper segregation and certified recycling to ensure zero e-waste reaches landfills.

💰

Valuable Resources

1 tonne of circuit boards contains 40-800x more gold than 1 tonne of ore. Recycling recovers copper, gold, silver & rare metals.

📊

India's E-Waste

India generates 3.2 million tonnes of e-waste annually — 3rd largest generator globally. Only 5% is formally recycled.

🤝

Join The Mission

Submit your e-waste to create jobs, reduce pollution and build a sustainable circular economy for India's future.

Impact

The Harmful Effects

Why proper e-waste disposal is critical for our planet and health

🏭

Air Pollution

Burning e-waste releases dioxins & furans — toxic pollutants causing respiratory diseases and cancer.

💧

Water Contamination

Heavy metals leach into groundwater, contaminating drinking water for millions of people.

🌱

Soil Degradation

Toxic substances destroy soil microorganisms, making land infertile and entering the food chain.

👶

Health Hazards

Children in e-waste areas show higher rates of DNA damage, learning disabilities and organ problems.
